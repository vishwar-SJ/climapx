# ClimapX 🌍

**AI-Powered Climate Safety Platform for India**

ClimapX is an intelligent environmental health platform designed to protect people across India from climate and pollution-related risks during daily life, travel, and emergencies.

## Features

- 🌫️ **Real-time Air Quality Monitoring** — AQI data with pollutant breakdown (PM2.5, PM10, O₃, NO₂, SO₂, CO)
- 🌡️ **Heat & Weather Tracking** — Temperature, humidity, feels-like, and rain detection
- 🗺️ **Interactive Risk Map** — AQI, heat, and flood overlays with safe zone visualization
- 🧠 **AI Exposure Score** — Personalized risk calculation based on location, time-of-day, and multi-hazard data
- 🛣️ **Smart Journey Planner** — AI-optimized routes avoiding pollution, heat, floods, and traffic
- 🔥 **NASA Wildfire Detection** — Real-time FIRMS satellite data for fire alerts
- 💧 **Water Quality Alerts** — Contamination risk warnings during heavy rainfall
- 🚗 **Traffic Pollution Alerts** — Rush-hour exposure warnings
- 🛡️ **Preventive AI Advice** — Actionable safety guidance (delay travel, wear N95, stay indoors, relocate)
- 🌙 **Dark Mode** — Full light/dark theme support
